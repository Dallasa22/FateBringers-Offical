About Us

FateBringers was founded off the idea that no one would have to play alone again. FateBringers is a Discord based clan. We use Discord to communicate, but we do communicate over each platform as well. We are an active & relatively large clan. Games we play include anything from Destiny 1 & 2, Call of Duty, PUBG & Fortnite, etc. We have members all across these games and across different platforms.

Our Role

Our role for the community is to be a welcoming and family like clan. Our goals are to grow in the gaming community, To be a supporting team, clan, & family to those in need. We encourage those who are seeking something greater, to join the fight. We look forward to playing with you!
